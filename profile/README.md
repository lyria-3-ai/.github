## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. 🖼️ True Multimodal Input

A signature feature that goes beyond text to read visual inspiration, composing a perfectly matched original soundtrack.

Upload a photo or video clip and [Lyria 3](https://lyria3.pro) interprets the visual elements—colors, composition, motion, and emotional tone—to automatically generate a cohesive audio track. 

### 2. 🎤 Auto-Lyrics & Realistic Vocals

Lyria 3 generates fully produced vocal performances instead of relying on external tools.

* **Autonomous Songwriting:** The model writes rhythmically coherent, rhyming lyrics based on your theme.
* **Multilingual Support:** Sing and rap authentically in 8+ languages (English, Chinese, Japanese, French, Spanish, etc.).
* **Natural Delivery:** Generates lifelike virtual vocals with natural pronunciation, zero robotic artifacts, and genre-appropriate delivery.

### 3. 🎧 Studio-Grade Audio Quality

Automatically generate complex, professional audio tracks including:

* **48kHz / 24-bit Stereo:** True high-fidelity audio generated directly from the foundational synthesis stage.
* **No Upsampling:** Rich, detailed soundstage without cheap mono-to-stereo conversion.
* **Precise Customization:** Specify exact tempo (BPM), dial in the mood, and choose from dozens of genres (Cinematic, EDM, Lo-Fi, Hip-Hop).
