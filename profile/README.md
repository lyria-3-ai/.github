## 🚀 What is Lyria 3?

[Lyria 3](https://lyria3.pro) is the premier online AI song and music generator powered by Google DeepMind's third-generation AI music model — the most advanced music generation system ever released. 

Built on latent diffusion architecture and trained on massive licensed datasets, Lyria 3 transforms simple text prompts, or even uploaded images and videos, into studio-grade songs complete with vocals, lyrics, and full instrumental production in seconds. No music theory or studio equipment required.

## ✨ Key Features

* 🖼️ **True Multimodal Input:** Go beyond text. Upload a photo or video clip and Lyria 3 reads the visual mood (colors, motion, emotion) to compose a perfectly matched original soundtrack.
* 🎤 **Auto-Lyrics & Realistic Vocals:** Automatically writes rhythmically coherent, rhyming lyrics. Features lifelike virtual vocals singing or rapping in 8+ languages (English, Chinese, Japanese, Spanish, French, Korean, etc.) with authentic pronunciation.
* 🎛️ **Precise Creative Control:** Set exact tempo (BPM), choose from dozens of genres (Pop, Hip-Hop, Cinematic, EDM, etc.), dial in the mood, and specify instrumentation. 
* 🎧 **Studio-Grade Audio Quality:** Native 48kHz/24-bit stereo output directly from the foundational synthesis stage. No cheap mono-to-stereo upsampling.
* ⏱️ **Optimized for Creators:** Generates high-density 30-second audio clips structurally optimized for short-form content (YouTube Shorts, TikTok, Instagram Reels).
* 🛡️ **100% Royalty-Free & Safe:** All tracks are yours to keep and use commercially without copyright claims. Includes built-in SynthID watermarking for AI identification.
